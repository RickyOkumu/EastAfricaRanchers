About East Africa Ranchers

Welcome to East Africa Ranchers

East Africa Ranchers is a dynamic agribusiness company dedicated to delivering high-quality, naturally raised livestock and fresh agricultural produce across East Africa.

We specialize in premium goats, sheep, and cattle — including live animals, top-grade mutton and beef — complemented by fresh vegetables, herbs, and pure natural honey sourced from unspoiled environments.

Our operations span Kenya including Kitengela, Rumuruti, Oloitoktok, Narok, Kajiado, and Wajir, supported by trusted partners in Tanzania, Uganda, and Ethiopia.

We combine traditional pastoral heritage with modern agribusiness to promote sustainability, food security, and regional prosperity.
